About Us

Welcome to SeaBreeze Initiative for sea life!

The SeaBreeze Animal Rehabilitation Program is an important part of SeaWorld's commitment to conservation, research, and education. Through this initiative, the SeaBreeze Initiative for sea life, treat, shelter, and release stranded animals. The main objective of the Endangered species Program is to help, rehabilitated and return animals to the wild. All SeaBreeze clubs work hard to help create better homes for marine life in need. The ability to rescue and rehabilitate animals is the result of years of experience caring volunteers year round.
